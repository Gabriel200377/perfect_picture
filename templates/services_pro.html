Planning Ahead

For quality assurance, please book your consultation 6 months prior to your event.

We can make exceptions for sooner events depending on our schedule. Share your date with us to see if fast-tracking is possible.

Consultations last for about 55 minutes or less per person.

Fees & Payments

Consultation fees must be paid before confirmation.

Fees are non-refundable and non-transferable.

We accept payments in Nigerian Naira and multiple global currencies.

Important Policies: Quality, Materials, and Terms

Fabric & Materials

For design integrity and craftsmanship standards, all materials are exclusively sourced and selected by Picture Perfect.

Clients may not supply external fabrics.

Deposits & Payment Terms

A 70% deposit is required to initiate production, 20% at first fitting and the final 10% settled before pickup or delivery.

Payments are accepted through our website or via secure direct payment links.

Changes, Refunds, and Exchanges

Picture Perfect does not offer refunds or exchanges after the purchase of fabric and production has commenced (maximum of 15 days after consultation).

Any change to the agreed-upon design, lining, fabric, or embellishment after production has commenced will incur a minimum charge of 40% of the initial production cost.

Fittings

Please prepare to come in for your fitting when you are not in a hurry; this is a couture service.

Fittings done after the close of work will attract extra charges.

Client Responsibility & Liability

For dresses made with illusion mesh or net fabrics(very delicate), clients are responsible for the integrity of the fabric once worn. Please ensure to follow our care guide for your dress attached, to enable use and reuse

Picture Perfect is not liable for any rips, tears, or damage caused during use.

Clients are responsible for the cost of any further adjustments needed after the garment has been worn.
